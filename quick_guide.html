The Buff Calculator helps you plan construction or research times by applying your total buff %, daily free speed-ups, and alliance helps.

Game Logic:
Your buffs reduce the total build time multiplicatively. After buffs are applied, daily speed-ups and alliance helps reduce the remaining time.

Formulas Used:

  • Buffed Time = Original Time √∑ (1 + Total Buff % as decimal)
  • Final Time = Buffed Time - (Daily Speed-Ups + Help Reduction)

Tip: Secretary of Development adds +50% to your buff % when active. This is automatically included if you add it to your buff total.



Buff Calculator Steps:

  1. Enter your original time (Days, Hours, Minutes, Seconds).
  2. Enter your total Buff % (including secretary buff %).
  3. Click "Calculate Buffed Time".
  4. Click "Apply Manual Speed-Ups & Help Reductions" (Optional).
  5. Enter your daily speed-up time, how many days to calculate, number of helps, and help-time per help.
  6. Click "Calculate Final Time" to see your real build time.
