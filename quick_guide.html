Buff Calculator Steps:

  1. Enter your original time (Days, Hours, Minutes, Seconds).
  2. Enter your total Buff % (including Secretary buff %).
  3. Click "Calculate Buffed Time".
  4. Click "Apply Manual Speed-Ups & Help Reductions" (optional).
  5. Enter your daily speed-up time, number of days to calculate, number of helps, and help-time per help.
  6. Click "Calculate Final Time".

Buff Formulas

StepFormulaDescription
1Buffed Time = Original Time ÷ (1 + Buff %)Applies buffs to time.
2Daily Speed-Up = Speed-Up seconds × DaysSpeed-up reduction.
3Help Reduction = Helps × Help secondsHelp reduction.
4Final Time = Buffed Time - ReductionsFinal adjusted time.

*Tip: Convert hours/minutes into seconds before calculating.*

RSS Production Calculator Steps:

  1. Add entries for each resource (Food, Iron, Gold).
  2. Enter Buff % for each resource.
  3. Enter number of Secretary of Interior sessions (optional).
  4. Click "Calculate All".

Production Formulas

StepFormulaDescription
1Base = Σ (Qty × Output/hr)Pre-buff output.
2Buffed = Base × (1 + Buff %)Apply buff.
3Boost = Buffed × 100%Secretary bonus.
4Daily = Buffed × 24 + BonusDaily production.
5Weekly/Monthly = Daily × 7/30Long-term output.
