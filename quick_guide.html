Buff Calculator Steps:

  1. Enter your original time (Days, Hours, Minutes, Seconds).
  2. Enter your total Buff % (including all bonuses).
  3. Click "Calculate Buffed Time".

RSS Production Calculator Steps:

  1. For each resource (Food, Iron, Gold), add one or more entries with Quantity and Output/hr.
  2. Enter your production Buff % for each resource.
  3. Enter how many Secretary of Interior sessions you use daily (optional).
  4. Click "Calculate All" to see hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly production.
