Buff Calculator – Quick Guide

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Enter your Original Upgrade Time (Days, Hours, Minutes, Seconds).


Step 2: Enter your total Construction Buff % (including all bonuses and Secretary of Development %).


Step 3: Click "Calculate Buffed Time".


Step 4: Click "Apply Manual Speed-Ups & Help Reductions" (Optional)


Step 5: Enter your daily speed-up time, how many days to calculate, number of helps, and help-time per help.


Step 6: Click "Calculate Final Time" to see your real build time.
