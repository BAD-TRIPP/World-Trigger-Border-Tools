Quick Guide


Buff Calculator Steps:

  1. Enter your original time (Days, Hours, Minutes, Seconds).
  2. Enter your total Buff % (including secretary buff %).
  3. Click "Calculate Buffed Time".
  4. Click "Apply Manual Speed-Ups & Help Reductions" (Optional).
  5. Enter your daily speed-up time, how many days to calculate, number of helps, and help-time per help.
  6. Click "Calculate Final Time" to see your real build time.

RSS Production Calculator Steps:

  1. For each resource (Food, Iron, Gold), add one or more entries with Quantity and Output/hr.
  2. Enter your production Buff % for each resource.
  3. Enter how many Secretary of Interior sessions you use daily (optional).
  4. Click "Calculate All" to see hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly production.

Step Formula Description
1. Buffed Time Buffed Time = Original Time ÷ (1 + Buff % as decimal) Calculates reduced time after applying total buff %.
2. Total Daily Speed-Up Total Daily Speed-Up = Daily Speed-Up (seconds) × Days Time reduced by daily speed-ups over days.
3. Total Help Reduction Total Help Reduction = Helps × Help Time per Help (seconds) Time saved from alliance helps.
4. Final Time Final Time = Buffed Time - (Total Speed-Ups + Total Help Reduction) Actual time remaining after all reductions.

*Tip: Convert hours, minutes, and seconds into total seconds before calculating.*
