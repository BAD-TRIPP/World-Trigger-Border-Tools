Step-by-Step

Step 1: Enter your Base Upgrade Time (Days, Hours, Minutes, Seconds).
Step 2: Enter your total Construction Buff % (including all bonuses and Secretary of Development).
Step 3: Click "Calculate Buffed Time".
Step 4: (Optional) Click "Apply Manual Speed-Ups & Help Reductions".
Step 5: Enter your daily speed-up time, days to calculate, number of helps, and help time per help.
Step 6: Click "Calculate Final Time" to see your real build time.
